About

HELLO

I am Enzo Ferrari, the founder of Scuderia Ferrari and Ferrari S.p.A.. My passion for speed and innovation shaped the world of motorsport. Born in 1898, I started as a racing driver before building legendary cars. I created Ferrari to dominate Formula 1 and craft the finest road machines. For me, a car is not just metal—it's emotion, power, and art. Even today, my legacy lives on in every prancing horse on the road.

Projects

The LaFerrari is Ferrari's first hybrid hypercar, combining V12 + electric power. Its 6.3L V12 + electric motor produces a massive 950 horsepower. With a top speed of 217 mph, it competes with the best hypercars ever made. Only 499 coupes + 210 Aperta versions exist, making it ultra-exclusive.

The Ferrari 250 GTO is the rarest and most valuable Ferrari ever made. Only 36 units were built, making it a collector's dream. It has a 3.0L V12 engine and was designed for racing dominance. One was sold for $70 million, making it the most expensive car in history.

The F40 was built to celebrate Ferrari's 40th anniversary. With a 2.9L twin-turbo V8, it was the first production car to exceed 200 mph, It was raw, lightweight, and built for pure speed, with no luxury features, Only 1,315 units were made, making it one of Ferrari's most iconic cars.
